Any macros in the url will be replaced with appropriate values before being sent to the tracker

Available macros:
* {window} - the id of the window
* {tab} - the id of the tab
* {action} - the action (eg. window_in for a window gaining focus)
* {timestamp} - the timestamp of a given event
* {url} - the url of the page that the browser was at when the action occured

A sample url: http://127.0.0.1:8000/tracker/{window}/{tab}/{action}/{timestamp}/{url}
